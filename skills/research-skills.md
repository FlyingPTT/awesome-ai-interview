# Research Skills

## Paper Reading Skill

Purpose: read a research paper and extract interview-useful insights.

Inputs:

- Paper title or PDF.
- Target role or topic.

Workflow:

1. Identify the research problem.
2. Summarize the core method.
3. Extract the key equation or algorithm.
4. Compare with prior work.
5. Identify limitations.
6. Convert the paper into interview talking points.

Output:

- One-sentence summary.
- Method breakdown.
- Strengths.
- Weaknesses.
- Implementation notes.
- Interview questions.

## Literature Survey Skill

Purpose: build a topic map from several papers.

Workflow:

1. Group papers by problem.
2. Group methods by architecture.
3. Track chronological development.
4. Identify open problems.
5. Produce a reading order.

Output:

- Topic taxonomy.
- Paper table.
- Trend summary.
- Recommended reading path.

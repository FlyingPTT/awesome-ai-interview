# Project: Paper Reading Assistant

## Goal

Build an assistant that reads AI papers and converts them into structured study notes, implementation insights, and interview questions.

## Architecture

- Paper metadata ingestion.
- PDF parsing.
- Section extraction.
- Method summarization.
- Related-work mapping.
- Interview question generation.

## Resume Bullet

Built a paper reading assistant that extracts problem formulation, method design, experimental results, limitations, and interview-ready talking points from AI research papers.

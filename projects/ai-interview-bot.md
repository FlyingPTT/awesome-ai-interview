# Project: AI Interview Simulator

## Goal

Build a simulator that asks adaptive LLM, RAG, and agent interview questions, scores answers, and gives targeted feedback.

## Architecture

- Question bank.
- Difficulty controller.
- Rubric-based evaluator.
- Follow-up question generator.
- Weakness tracker.
- Study plan generator.

## Resume Bullet

Developed an AI interview simulator with adaptive question selection, rubric-based answer evaluation, follow-up probing, and personalized study plan generation for LLM engineering interviews.
